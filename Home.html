Are Edge Weights in Summary Graphs Us​eful? 
A Comparative Study of Graph Summarization Models

Overview

We conduct a systematic compariso​n between a weighted graph summarization model and an unweighted graph summarization model.
Our contributions are as follow:

  • Systematic Comparsion: coductiong the first systematic comparison between two extensively-studied graph summarization models.
  • Unexpected Observation: leading to a surprising observation that unweighted models are significantly better than weighted models.
  • Improvement of State-of-the-art Algorithms: improving a state-of-the-art graph-summarizaion algorithm for the weighted model by adapting it to the unweighted model.
